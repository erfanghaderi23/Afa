How We Begin

Amir Azerbaijan Steel Company as a pioneer private company in the production of steel ingots on a land area of ​​about 10 hectares in 1400 in Bostan Abad city with the aim of creating employment for 300 people directly and producing 500 thousand tons of steel ingots, has been established. Using the latest technology of induction furnaces, ladle furnaces and continuous casting machines, this factory has the ability to produce various types of construction and alloy steel ingots with sizes of 120 * 120 to 200 * 200 in different lengths. The company uses 6 induction furnaces of 40 tons with the latest technology in this field to prepare melt from scrap iron and sponge iron and 4-line continuous casting machines, puts the desired quality at the forefront and with the latest laboratory equipment and also The use of experienced manpower is the foremost quality production in the region.
